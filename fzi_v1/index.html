A brief welcome address by the ED TO THE WEBSITE, pictures of conferences or seminars attended

Hunger, poverty and illiteracy progressively expresses themselves as you move inward from urban to rural communities in Nigeria. The enormousity of their silent outcry of poverty speaks volume day in and day out.
As an organization, we understand that response to the “outcry of the poor cannot wait”, because of the social implications involve. Through our project, Frontida Zois Initiative operate to eliminate the short and long terms cost of inaction resulting from low response to the needs of the poor and vulnerable- we are responding to cost of inaction resulting problems such as hooliganism, insurgencies, kidnapping, arm robbery, fraud, and social ills in Nigeria. .
Ultimately, our mission at Frontida Zois Initiative aim to enable the future of Nigeria and Africa at large to be well cared for, protected and empowered to nurture economic and productive citizens of tomorrow.

VISION

"Combat hunger, poverty and illiteracy in communities."

MISSION

Educate, empower and conduct researches to enhance the lives of young people, households and communities sustainably as well as mobilize resources to tackle critical societal issues and foster economic growth.

GOAL

To enlighten, guide and empower human productivity, enhance peace process among families and promote development of communities through dialogue, advocacy and empowerment.
